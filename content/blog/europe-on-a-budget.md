# Europe on a Budget: How Indian Travellers Can Save Big

Europe is a dream destination for millions of Indian travellers — but the perception of high costs often holds people back. The truth? With smart planning, Europe can be surprisingly affordable.

## Travel in the Shoulder Season

Avoid July–August peak season. **April–June** and **September–October** offer nearly identical weather with significantly lower hotel prices and shorter queues at major attractions.

**Pro tip:** April in Paris is magical — the cherry blossoms are in bloom, temperatures are pleasant, and prices are 30–40% lower than July.

## Money-Saving Tips on the Ground

1. **Eat where locals eat** — Step one block away from tourist squares and prices halve
2. **City museum passes** — Paris Museum Pass and Roma Pass save ₹3,000–5,000 per person
3. **Eurocoach over trains** — For inter-city travel on group tours, coach is included and saves significantly
4. **Free attractions** — Many world-class museums are free on the first Sunday of each month
5. **Water** — Tap water is safe to drink across most of Europe; carry a reusable bottle
